Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Quick Summary: Cancel 24+ hours before = Full refund. Cancel within 24 hours = No refund. Doctor cancellation = Full refund.

1. Cancellation by Patient

1.1 Full Refund (100%)

  • Cancellation made more than 24 hours before scheduled appointment
  • Technical issues preventing consultation (verified by support team)
  • Doctor unavailability or cancellation

1.2 No Refund

  • Cancellation within 24 hours of scheduled appointment
  • Patient no-show (missed appointment without cancellation)
  • Consultation already completed

2. Cancellation by Doctor

If a doctor cancels an appointment for any reason:

  • 100% refund processed automatically
  • Option to reschedule with same or different doctor
  • Refund credited to original payment method within 5-7 business days

3. Refund Processing Time

  • Wallet/UPI: Instant to 24 hours
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • Net Banking: 5-7 business days

4. Partial Consultation

If a consultation is interrupted or incomplete due to technical issues:

  • Report within 1 hour of scheduled time
  • Support team reviews case
  • Partial or full refund based on consultation duration
  • Alternative: Free rescheduling

5. How to Request Refund

  1. Go to "My Bookings" in the app
  2. Select the appointment
  3. Click "Request Refund"
  4. Provide reason (if required)
  5. Submit request

6. Non-Refundable Items

  • Platform service fees (if applicable)
  • Payment gateway charges
  • Promotional discounts (credited back as platform credits)

7. Dispute Resolution

If you have concerns about a refund:

  • Email: refunds@doctorhelp.com
  • Phone: +91-1800-XXX-XXXX
  • Response time: Within 48 hours
  • Resolution time: 5-7 business days

8. Policy Updates

This refund policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or app notification.

9. Contact

For refund inquiries: support@doctorhelp.com
